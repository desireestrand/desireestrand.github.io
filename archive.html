    Webb | Alien Maze, ett objektorienterat labyrintspel. Skoluppgift från 2020, skapad med p5.js tillsammans med Ottilia Olsson.

      Archive

      In the archive you find short school projects and other personal design experiments.

      Web | Interactive generative art with repetition of graphic shapes, a paraphrase of Victor Vasarely's artwork. School assignment from 2020, created with p5.js together with Ottilia Olsson.

      Graphic | Olivia Strand Keramiks logotype. School assignment from 2019, created with Adobe Illustrator.

      Web | Graphic elements that respond to Swan Lake by Tchaikovsky. School assignment from 2020, created with p5.js together with Ottilia Olsson.

      Graphic | Art Deco-style poster for a conceptual seminar series on the theme of UN's global goals. School assignment from 2020, created with Adobe Illustrator.

      Web | My first website. School assignment from 2019, created with HTML and CSS.

      Graphic | Flyer layout. School assignment from 2019, created with Adobe InDesign.

      Graphic | Cover for Colby Brock's Spotify playlist, winner of a Heard Well contest 2019. Created with Adobe Photoshop.

      Web | Alien Maze, an object oriented maze game. School assignment from 2020, created with p5.js together with Ottilia Olsson.

      Graphic | Conceptual newspaper advertisement for GQ's interview with Diplo. Created in 2020 with Adobe Photoshop.

      Graphic | Conceptual graphic profile and advertising poster for the e-sport community Female Legends. School assignment from 2020, created with Adobe Illustrator and InDesign together with Lejla Bajramovic, Anne Dillström, Emma Sjökvist and Ottilia Olsson.

      Webb | Med världen i dina händer, a data visualisation. School assignment from 2020, created with p5.js and Techable Machine together with Ottilia Olsson.
